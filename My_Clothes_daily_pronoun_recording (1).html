Revision - My Clothes, colours and Phonograms

Pre A1 Cambridge Young Learners · UK English · 1-week home practice
我的衣服、颜色和拼读复习

📋 How to use · 使用说明

Spend about 15 minutes each day. Open the Daily Activities tab and complete only that day's tasks. Children should use picture → word → writing. Spelling has two parts: first listen to the letters, then write the whole word from memory only. Do not copy the word. 每天练习约15分钟。请打开 Daily Activities 标签，只完成当天任务。孩子要按 看图→认词→写词 的顺序学习。拼写分成两部分：先听字母，再凭记忆写完整单词。不要抄写。

Worksheets in daily order

Picture → word → writing · memory spelling · numbers 1–20

📅 Monday worksheet - Clothes memory spelling · 第1天 衣服记忆拼写

Look at the picture and read the word. Then hide the word and write it from memory. Do not copy. 先看图和单词，再把单词遮住，凭记忆写出来。不要抄写。

📅 Tuesday worksheet - Missing letters · 第2天 缺字母

Write the missing letter or letters inside the word. 在单词里填写缺少的字母。

🔢 Numbers 1–20 writing · 数字1到20书写

Write the number words. Then use them in age, quantity and counting sentences. 写数字单词，然后在年龄、数量和数数句子中使用。

📅 Wednesday worksheet - Jumbled words · 第3天 乱序单词

Look at the picture and unscramble the word. 看图，把乱序字母拼成正确单词。

Choose the correct sentence. 选择正确的句子。
